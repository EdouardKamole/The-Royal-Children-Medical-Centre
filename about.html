About RCMC.

The Royal Children’s Medical Centre Ltd is a private health service provider registered by the Uganda Medical and dental practitioner’s council located along Gayaza road highway at mile 6 (ku mukaaga), and a second branch at Kyengera on Kyengera mall The facility is the only Paediatric Specialised health care provider in the area and has full time specialist paediatrician coverage 24 hours a day 7 days a week. We are specialists in children (0 – 12 years) and adolescent health (10 – 17 years). The facility is manned by a team of 8 Paediatrician doctors. We have been running for the past 5 years and we have at all times adequately met the demands of our clients.

Because of the uniqueness and quality of care at the facility, we have attracted partnership with the key Insurance companies in Uganda. The facility so far accepts insurance cards from UAP Insurance, Jubilee, Liberty, Case Medicare, APA Insurance, AAR Insurance, ICEA, GA Insurance, Prudential Insurance, Sanlam Insurance, and corporate companies like Bank of Uganda. We also have arrangements to open to other international insurance providers such as CIGNA, and other corporate companies like NWSC, CIA, JMS, and others. We are also running a partnership with the Lancet Laboratories.

Our MISSION

To improve the health and wellbeing of Children and Adolescents by being an outstanding provider of quality child health services within and beyond Kampala. We will establish a regional child health care system of prevention, promote a family centred system of care and prevention, engage in generation of new knowledge relevant to child health and wellbeing, and advocate for programs which will benefit children, families, and communities

Our VISION




To be the Leading Innovative service provider in Paediatric Health care through our unsurpassed quality Clinical care, Compassion for Children and their Familie
