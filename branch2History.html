Our Branch History

A Journey of Compassionate Pediatric Care

2022

Branch Establishment

Founded by Dr. Elizabeth Namukasa to extend pediatric healthcare to Eastern Uganda. Initial services launched with a focus on community health.

2023

Expanding Diagnostic Capabilities

Invested in state-of-the-art imaging equipment to provide advanced diagnostic services for children in the region.

2024

Specialized Clinic Expansion

Introduced specialized pediatric subspecialty clinics including Pediatric Cardiology, Neonatology, and Child Nutrition Programs.

Our Founding Vision

To provide accessible, advanced, and compassionate pediatric healthcare to underserved communities in Eastern Uganda.

Community Impact
  • Reduced child mortality rates
  • Free immunization programs
  • Specialized pediatric care
